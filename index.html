🛡️ What is ParentShield?

ParentShield is a local-first documentation app designed to help parents keep clear, organized records of important parenting events. It helps users create logs, attach supporting files, protect records inside a secure vault, and export documentation when needed.

ParentShield is built for clarity, privacy, and real-world use. Your records stay on your device unless you choose to export them.

🎯 Why ParentShield Exists

ParentShield was created to help parents document important events in one secure place. In stressful situations, details get lost, timelines get blurry, and records become hard to organize. ParentShield gives users a structured way to keep track of what happened and when.

The app is designed to help users:

  • Create organized parenting records
  • Add attachments to support a record
  • Protect sensitive information in a secure vault
  • Export records for personal reference, legal preparation, or professional review

ParentShield focuses on practical documentation, privacy, and control.

  • Court-ready documentation
  • Timestamped incident logging
  • Encrypted, tamper-proof records
  • Visual and digital evidence vaulting
  • AI-powered legal log summaries
  • Secure PIN-based access
  • Manual upload (no automatic cloud sync)

This tool doesn’t just help you keep track—it helps you build a defense.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026

ParentShield is a local-first documentation app developed by Ninja Riot Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how ParentShield handles user information.

Information storage. ParentShield stores records, drafts, finalized entries, and related attachments on the user’s device. ParentShield does not automatically upload user records to external servers or cloud services by default.

Data collection. ParentShield does not collect personal data for analytics, advertising, or user tracking. ParentShield does not sell user data.

Data sharing. ParentShield does not share user data with third parties by default. Data is only exported or shared when the user chooses to export or share records.

Security. ParentShield is designed with local data protection in mind and includes secure vault functionality for sensitive records.

User control. Users control their records on their own device. Users may delete app data, remove records through app features where available, or remove stored data by deleting the app’s local data from their device.

Children’s data. ParentShield is a recordkeeping tool for parents and guardians. It is not designed as a standalone service for children to use directly.

Changes to this policy. This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.

Contact. For questions about this Privacy Policy or ParentShield, contact Ninja Riot Studio through the contact information listed on this website.

🔐 Privacy & Data

ParentShield is designed with a local-first approach. Records are stored on the user's device, and sensitive vault data is protected within the app.

Category Stored Locally Notes
Draft records ✅ Saved for later editing inside the app
Finalized records ✅ Kept for reference and export
Attachments ✅ Stored on device and associated with records
Exports ✅ Created only when the user chooses to export

ParentShield does not use ads or tracking, and it does not automatically upload user records to outside services.
