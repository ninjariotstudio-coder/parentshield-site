🛡️ What is ParentShield?

ParentShield is a secure, local-first evidence vault built for parents navigating high-conflict custody battles, court disputes, or co-parenting struggles. It’s more than an app—it’s a digital weapon for truth, accountability, and protection. Born out of lived experience and necessity, ParentShield gives parents a way to document, organize, encrypt, and defend their parenting journey without relying on cloud services or broken systems.

Everything stays on your device—unless you choose to upload it.

🎯 Why ParentShield Exists

Most family court systems don’t protect good parents. Evidence gets ignored. Lies go unchecked. And fathers especially are often forced to prove they’re fit when they’ve done nothing wrong.

ParentShield was created for people in that fight.

The app empowers users with:

  • Court-ready documentation
  • Timestamped incident logging
  • Encrypted, tamper-proof records
  • Visual and digital evidence vaulting
  • AI-powered legal log summaries
  • Secure PIN-based access
  • Manual upload (no automatic cloud sync)

This tool doesn’t just help you keep track—it helps you build a defense.

🧠 The Core Architecture (NinjaCore)

At the heart of ParentShield is NinjaCore, a modular system designed to handle high-conflict logging, military-grade encryption, and flexible expansion into AI, automation, and decentralized storage.

NinjaCore Highlights:

EncryptionServiceHelper: Centralized encryption/decryption that keeps sensitive logs secure (text-based only; images/PDFs stay unencrypted for legal compatibility).

EncryptedMetaService: Handles metadata indexing for secure logs, used across all vault types.

Modular Vault Screens: Separate screens for Legal Logs, Visitation Logs, Communication, Child Support, and Digital Evidence.

First-time Setup Flow: Requires PIN + at least 1 parent + 1 child profile before anything else loads.

No Google, Firebase, or 3rd party tracking: Fully independent. Full control.

🔐 What’s Stored?
